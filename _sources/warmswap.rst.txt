
Warm swap
===================================================================

1. Login to Shaheen-II
    `$ ssh <user_name>@shaheen.hpc.kaust.edu.sa`
2. Login to the Shaheen-II Cray System Management Workstation (smw2)
    `$ ssh crayadm@smw2`
3. Halt the blade
    `xtcli halt <blade_id>`
